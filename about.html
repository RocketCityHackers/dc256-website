About DC256

DC256 is the official DEF CON Group for Huntsville, Alabama. We're a gathering of hackers, infosec professionals, makers, tinkerers, and curious minds who share a passion for cybersecurity, digital privacy, hardware hacking, and open knowledge.

We host monthly meetups, workshops, Capture The Flag (CTF) competitions, and talks ranging from beginner-friendly introductions to advanced topics. Whether you're just getting started or you're a seasoned hacker, DC256 is a welcoming place to learn and connect.

We believe in curiosity, creativity, community — and of course, breaking things (ethically). Join us and be part of Huntsville's growing hacker culture.
